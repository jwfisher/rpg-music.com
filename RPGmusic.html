Welcome to the RPG Music WebRing Home Page!

Do you have a webpage that has RPG music/sound files? Then why don't you join? We'd be happy to have you in the ring! Your webpage must meet only a few requirements..
  1. Have a organized website. Be able to update it at least once in a while.
  2. Have the webring code either on the page that has your RPG Music files, your main page, or a webring page. Basicly, have it visable and in a reasonable place.
  3. Download the webring pictures, please don't link to mine.
  4. Please do not change or edit the code or the pictures.
  5. No linking to other peoples music. Be original! Don't just have one music file.
  6. And finally, please no adult content websites. This webring is for the general audience.
Would you like to join? Just do these 3 steps:
  1. Download and upload to your website the two webring graphics (RPGmusic.jpg and RPGnext.jpg). Just right click them and click either "Save Image As" or "Save Target As" (dependant on which browser you have.
  2. Insert the HTML code that is included in the e-mail you got when you submitted, all your information should be there.
