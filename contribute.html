Contribute to the RPG Music Dimension!



I have decided to create a separate page for this. The main reason is I would like to see more participation. One reason why that might be is that I don't get that many visitors, which I hope to remedy this summer. I need to advertise more. But anyway, back to the subject ~_^.

Here's what I'm looking for:

  • Midis
    The midis can be for any RPG game for any platform. As you can probably see, I didn't have any midis from sega systems, until today, I hope to increase that this summer! They can be just like the game, arranged, enhanced, etc. I will even take midi mixes of several songs from a single game or spanning games. I haven't open a page for that, but I will in the near future.
  • Digital Music
    This is music or the form .it .s3m .xm etc. Same here goes as above. This archive is small right now, but I will make it bigger in the future.
  • mp3s
    Now, when I say mp3, I don't mean right from the game...(not the most legal ya know ^_^). When I say mp3 I mean pieces you created. This can be of the form of instrumental, synthesized, or any other type that was created by your hands/mouth, etc.

Now, for any piece that you create, I will credit you in my credits page. Or if you're sending me someone else's work, tell me who made it. If this starts to get really long, I may change it to a different system in the future. So e-mail me at jwfisher@iastate.edu if you would like to contribute to this website (which I hope you do! ~_~).
