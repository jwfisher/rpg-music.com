Here is a short description of "The Infinite War Within." I would highly recommend reading the archives. Someday soon, I will take the archives and convert them to a story with chapters.


Kamain Kelineva, a small town tavern bouncer, discovers he has an "infinite war" within him, an angel and a devil. It has been passed down through the generations of time. When this is discovered, he begins on a quest to rid of this war. On the way, he becomes friends with several people. Lin, a fighter mage princess; Sage and Jewel, father and daughter mage, and many others. Can Kamain get rid of the devil and rid of this war forever? Read the archives below!
