Weird Al Yankovic!!

I have listened to Weird Al for a long time now. I have quite a few of his albums. If you count mp3s, I think I might have 90% of his songs! He mainly changes the words of famous songs; but he does make a few original pieces too. This picture above is promoting Weird Al's CD "Bad Hair Day". It was released about 4 years ago. His latest album, "Running with Scissors" is a blast. Gotta love "The Saga Begins", "Albuquerque", and "It's All about the Pentiums." In August of '96 I went to one of his concerts of the tour "Bad Hair Day". That was awesome. Then just this February I went with a big group of friends to his "Touring with Scissors". Damn, that was so much fun!
