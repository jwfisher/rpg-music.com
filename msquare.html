My rating system is a rating of 1-5. You need the font "Wingdings" to view my rating system. You should have it; if you don't you'll see "C"s. It is based upon my opinon on how close the midi is to the actual game if it was not a remix/arrangement. It is qualtity when it is a remix and/or arrangement. This ratings are only my opinion. The way the midi sounds depends greatlyon your sound card. The rating is as follows:
C Barely recognizable
CC fair
CCC okay
CCCC Very good
CCCCCIf not perfect, very close!
´ I haven't heard that song yet/haven't heard it enough to make judgement
All these games either have been released in the US or going to be released in the near future. Midis from games I have not played, I will leave the Rating blank.
