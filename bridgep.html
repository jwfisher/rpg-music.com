The Bridge

Prologue


This world is vast. There are two mainlands on this planet. Between them is a vast ocean. You might consider this planet to be a solid Jupiter. By some outside force, one of the mainlands was frozen in time, kept from evolving, and yet the other mainland on the planet was able to thrive. The west mainland was frozen in time for 500 years, during the Medieval era. This is a story of the two mainlands....discovering....
